Yoga Classes

Gentle Hatha Yoga

Intended for beginners and anyone wishing a grounded foundation in the practice of yoga, this 60 minutes class of poses and slow movements focuses on asana (proper alignment and posture), pranyama (breath work), and guided meditation to foster your mind and your body connection.

Vinyasa Yoga

Althrough designed for intermediate to advanced students, beginners are welcome to sample this 60 minutes class that focuses on breath-synchronized movement — you will inhale and exhale as you flow energitically through yoga poses.

Restoration Yoga

This 90 minutes class feature very slow movements and long poses that are supported by a chair or a wall. This calming, restorative experience is suitable for students of any level of experience. This practice can be a perfect way to help rehabilitate an injury.
